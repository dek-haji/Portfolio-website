Full-stack developer based out of Nashville

Two years ago, while I was busy preparing for my A+ certification exam I came across a free CS50 Computer Science Courses Program from Harvard. After completing the first course and writing my first HELLO WORLD code, I realized how much I enjoyed coding. Without hesitation, I applied for the Nashville Community College, majoring in Computer Information Technology. Degree in hand, I felt ready to build my skills with hands-on coding experience by taking additional online courses. Longing to connect with like-minded people, I researched ways to further pursue my education and discovered a community of folks with similar interests as mine at the Nashville Software School (NSS) and applied their to try my hand at becoming a Full Stack .NET C# Software Developer. After completing a competitive application/interview process, I was lucky enough to join the NSS family.
