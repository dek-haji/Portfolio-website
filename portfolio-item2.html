The title Ask App

Ask and Get Help

ASK is a platform for students and alumni to share their questions and get help.

A student can review the list of previous student questions, and if they can't find their answer they can submit their questions. And the Alumni can check all the list of questions or search the specific question types that the alumni are familiar to and not answered yet the Alumni can support the student and respond to their inquiries.

Technologies

  • C#
  • Entity Framework
  • Identity Framework
  • SQL
  • Bootstrap 4
